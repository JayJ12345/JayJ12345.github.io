Vocab Words for English

Hover under the vocab word for the answer.

Dialect
A way of speaking that is particularly to a specific place. Its words, the way those words are said, and/or the spelling of those words are slightly different from those used by other people who speak the same language.
Hyperbole
A type of figurative language that uses extreme exaggeration to make a point.
Irony
A contradiction between what is expected and what actually happens.
Idiom
An expression, figure of speech, or specialized vocabulary particular to a language, region, or group.
Literary Nonfiction
Writing that uses narrative techniques to convey factual information.
Subjective
Shaped by personal bias.
Understatement
A statement that is expressed with restraint or represents something as less important than it really is.
Objective
Without opinion or bias.
